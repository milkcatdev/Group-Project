Now an amber brown colour, her fur sits frizzed along the fresh bedsheet, a faint yellow hue barely visible on parts of her legs.
She sits still and waiting, her limbs, weak, yet hold a strong attachment to her body.
'She's alive', a girl used to say promisingly, almost convincingly. Someone took great care of her. 
Though melancholy looms within her, as if waiting for a girl to come back home.
